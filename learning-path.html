FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our programs

How long is each session?

Each session is 60 minutes, divided into 45 minutes of instruction and 15 minutes of review and assessment.

Can I reschedule a session?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before the session time by contacting your teacher directly.

What payment methods are available?

We accept Vodafone Cash, bank transfer, and cash payment at our academy location.

Is there a free trial session?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute trial session to assess your level and familiarize you with our teaching method.

What are the requirements for an Ijaza?

You must complete full Quran memorization, master Tajweed, and pass both oral and written examinations.
