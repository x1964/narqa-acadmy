Our Story

Connecting hearts to the Quran,
one student at a time

Founded in 2014, Narqa Academy was born from a simple mission: to make authentic Islamic education accessible to every Muslim, wherever they are in the world.

Our Mission

Education rooted in
authentic tradition

At Narqa Academy, we believe that access to authentic Islamic knowledge should never be limited by geography, time zones, or language barriers. Our founders — all scholars with Ijazah — built this platform to preserve the sacred chain of knowledge while embracing modern technology.

Today we serve over 12,000 students across 40 countries. From children memorising their first juz to adults seeking Ijazah certification, Narqa Academy has guided them all with care, structure, and sincerity.
