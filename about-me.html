Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the world's leading reference in online Quran education, and to contribute to graduating one million Hafiz and Hafiza by 2030.

  • Universal access to digital Quranic education
  • Interactive electronic curricula development
  • A globally connected Quranic community
  • Leadership in Quran education research

Our Mission

To facilitate learning and teaching the Quran with high quality through advanced electronic platforms, focusing on building the spiritual bond between student and Allah's book.

  • Distinguished Quranic education with modern methods
  • Enabling Muslims to memorize and master Tajweed anywhere
  • Qualifying outstanding Quran teachers
  • Spreading knowledge linked to the Holy Quran

Narqa Academy Timeline

A story of giving and growth since our launch, steadily marching toward our grand goals

2021

The First Launch

Founded Narqa Academy with 5 teachers and 50 students, starting lessons via Zoom.
